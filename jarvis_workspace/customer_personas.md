# Customer Personas for Jarvis AI Co-Founder

## Persona 1: Early-Stage Startup Founder
- **Demographics**: 25-35 years old, tech-savvy, bootstrapped
- **Pain Points**: Limited resources, need for 24/7 support, lack of executive experience
- **Goals**: Scale quickly, reduce operational overhead, make smart decisions
- **Budget**: $500-$2000/month

## Persona 2: Remote Team Manager
- **Demographics**: 30-45 years old, distributed team leader
- **Pain Points**: Communication gaps, time zone challenges, decision fatigue
- **Goals**: Improve team coordination, make data-driven decisions, reduce meeting overhead
- **Budget**: $1000-$3000/month

## Persona 3: Growth Hacker
- **Demographics**: 25-40 years old, focused on rapid scaling
- **Pain Points**: Need for constant innovation, resource constraints, market analysis
- **Goals**: Identify opportunities, optimize processes, accelerate growth
- **Budget**: $1500-$4000/month
